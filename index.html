Foo fighters
Brief Biography: Founded by Dave Grohl in 1994, Foo Fighters are known for their energetic rock sound and anthems blending alternative and post-grunge styles.
Most Representative Album: The Colour and the Shape (1997) - Featuring hits like "Everlong" and "My Hero."
Muse
Brief Biography: British rock band Muse, formed in 1994, blends alternative rock with classical and electronic elements, known for their dramatic live shows.
Most Representative Album: Absolution (2003) - Includes tracks like "Time Is Running Out" and "Hysteria."
Radiohead
Brief Biography: UK band Radiohead, formed in 1985, is known for their experimental sound combining alternative, electronic, and ambient elements.
Most Representative Album: Absolution (2003) - Includes tracks like "Time Is Running Out" and "Hysteria."
Current joys
Brief Biography: Solo project of Nicholas Rattigan, known for its lo-fi indie rock with introspective, emotional lyrics.
Most Representative Album: Wild Heart (2018) - Highlights tracks like "New Flesh" and "My Willing Heart."
Nirvana
Brief Biography: Grunge pioneers fronted by Kurt Cobain, reshaping rock with their raw sound and impactful lyrics.
Most Representative Album: Nevermind (1991) - Includes iconic tracks like "Smells Like Teen Spirit" and "Come as You Are."
